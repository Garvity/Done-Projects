LeafLogic

Our platform aims to provide users with an easy way to upload images and analyze the results through predictions, helping users understand key insights and causes.

After clicking "Get Started," you can easily upload an image by selecting the "Upload Image" option. Then, simply click the "Predict" button to analyze the image.

After the analysis is completed, the results will be displayed on the screen with the causes and details about the prediction.

Currently, the platform supports one image prediction at a time. However, we are working on future updates to allow batch uploads.
